Welcome to Food Cafe

"Welcome to FOOD CAFE, where every meal is a delightful experience! Our food cafe offers a cozy atmosphere and a menu packed with delicious options that cater to all tastes and preferences. Whether you're here for a quick bite, a leisurely meal, or a sweet treat, we've got something special for you."


At FOOD CAFE, we believe in the joy of great food and the comfort of a welcoming atmosphere. Located in the heart of Matara, our cafe is the perfect spot to enjoy a delicious meal, catch up with friends, or simply relax with a cup of coffee.

Pizza

Indulge in our wide variety of pizzas, each crafted with the freshest ingredients and baked to perfection. From classic Margherita to gourmet toppings, our pizzas are a must-try.

Rice and Pasta

Savor the flavors of our exquisite rice and pasta dishes. Whether you crave a comforting bowl of risotto, a hearty plate of spaghetti, or a zesty stir-fry, our menu has it all.

Desserts

Treat yourself to our delectable desserts. From rich, creamy cheesecakes to light and fluffy pastries, our dessert menu is designed to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Beverages

Quench your thirst with our selection of beverages. Enjoy a refreshing soda, a hot cup of coffee, or a specialty drink crafted by our skilled baristas.
